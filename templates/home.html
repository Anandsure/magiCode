Learn How To Use magiCode


1) Choose whether you want to Practice/ Compete/ Redeem Hackos (Hackerrank points) from the menu.
   -If you choose to Practice a new language, click on the microphone button at the bottom of the white box.
   -Choose a language you need help with.
   -Ask magiCode what you need generally like 'i need an if condition' , 'perform addition for me' (syntaxes not required).
   - Copy the code from the white box into the input side of the compiler (left).
   -Run it and see the output on the right side of the compiler.

2) You can compete to score points in our Hackerrank contest.

3) You can redeem earned points to buy yourself something you like in the redeem section.
